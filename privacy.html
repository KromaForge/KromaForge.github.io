Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

🔒 The Short Version

KromaForge does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Period. Your images never leave your device, and we have zero tracking or analytics on this site.

No Data Collection

KromaForge is a fully client-side application. All image processing — cropping, resizing, filtering, drawing, and every other tool — happens entirely within your web browser using HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript. No data is ever sent to any external server.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device fingerprints or identifiers
  • IP addresses or geolocation data

Your Images Stay on Your Device

When you upload an image to KromaForge, it is loaded directly into your browser's memory. The image is never transmitted over the internet, uploaded to a cloud server, or shared with any third party. All editing operations are performed locally on your machine.

No Cookies

KromaForge does not use cookies of any kind — no session cookies, no tracking cookies, no third-party cookies. We have absolutely no cookie consent banner because there are no cookies to consent to.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or analytics service. There are no scripts on this site that monitor your behavior, track page views, or build advertising profiles.

Local Storage (IndexedDB)

KromaForge uses your browser's built-in IndexedDB to temporarily store your working image during an editing session. This allows you to move between tools without re-uploading your image.

This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Can be cleared at any time by you via the app's "Clear" button or your browser settings
  • Is automatically managed by your browser's storage policies

Third-Party Services

KromaForge loads fonts from Google Fonts. This is the only external request made by the application. Google's privacy policy applies to their font delivery service. No other third-party services are used.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given our commitment to zero data collection, we don't anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to open an issue on our GitHub repository.
