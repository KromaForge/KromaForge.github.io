About KromaForge

A premium suite of image editing tools that respects your privacy and runs entirely in your browser.

🔷 What is KromaForge?

KromaForge is a free, browser-based image editor that gives you access to 10 professional-grade tools — from cropping and resizing to pixel art conversion and meme generation. Unlike traditional online editors, KromaForge processes everything locally on your device. No server uploads, no sign-ups, no waiting.

Simply open the app, drop in your image, and start editing. It's that simple.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe image editing should be private by default. In a world where most "free" online tools quietly upload your photos to remote servers, KromaForge takes a different approach: your images never leave your device.

Our mission is to provide powerful, accessible image editing tools that anyone can use without sacrificing their privacy or paying a subscription fee. No ads, no upsells, no data harvesting — just tools that work.

⚡ Technology

KromaForge is built with modern web standards for maximum performance and compatibility:

HTML5 Canvas JavaScript (ES6+) IndexedDB CSS3 Animations Web APIs Glassmorphism UI

All image manipulation is performed using the HTML5 Canvas API, giving you GPU-accelerated processing without any server round-trips. IndexedDB provides seamless session persistence so you can switch between tools without losing your work.
